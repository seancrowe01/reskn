# Samples

Finished-by-robot example sites. These show what a typical draft looks like before Giezel polishes it.

These are NOT real client deliverables. They are reference examples only.

## What's in here

| Folder | Lead type | Notes |
|--------|-----------|-------|
| `ards-comber-boiler-services/` | HOT (no website) | Generated from scratch from Google Maps data only. 80 reviews, 5 stars. |

## How to look at one

Just double-click the `index.html` file. It opens in your browser.

To see the code, open the same file in your editor.

## What to look at

- Notice the 9 sections (nav, hero, trust strip, services, why us, reviews, service area, CTA banner, footer)
- All Tailwind CSS, no build step
- Stock photos from Unsplash that will be swapped for real client photos
- Placeholder reviews with link to real Google reviews
- Phone number is a clickable `tel:` link on every CTA
